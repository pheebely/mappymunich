MAPPY MUNICH

About

  Mappy Munich is an online guide that provides accessibility information for tourists with limited mobility. It provides accessibility information for essential amenities, and tourist attractions with self-guided tour routes in the city center of Munich.

  Note: For City Tours, if an attraction is wheelchair accessible, it is also assisted walking (e.g., rollator) and stroller friendly. If they are not wheelchair friendly, it is indicated whether the attraction is accessible for people with assisted walking or stroller.

DISCLAIMER

This website is for informational use only.
Please plan your travels according to your needs and safety first.
The accessibility information for attractions were compiled from official City of Munich websites and brochures with barrier-free information. Information on problem areas along the routes were downloaded from OpenStreetMap and processed with GIS software. The information we are providing was updated on January 2023 and the selection of accessible tourist attractions on this website is not exhaustive.
