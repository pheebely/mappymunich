MAPPY MUNICH

ABOUT

Mappy Munich is an online tourist guide intended for wheelchair users and people with limited mobility that provides accessibility information for essential amenities and tourist attractions with self-guided tour routes in the city of Munich.



We focused on the following users:
wheelchair users, users with assisted walking, and users with strollers.
